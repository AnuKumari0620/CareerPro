Full Job Description

Magnon Group is hiring a Cloud Engineer.

Experience Required: 3-10 Yrs.

Roles and Responsibilities:
  • Collaborate with the global engineering/development team, cultivating a robust and collaborative working environment
  • Provide input for systems architecture design and application architecture
  • Support the development teams in developing and troubleshooting the platform
  • Plan, develop, tested, and deploy updates to the infrastructure
  • Take ownership of deployments of code to our platforms
  • Help monitor and maintain the storage platform across our sites
  • Identify areas where existing infrastructure and practices can be improved and work to implement those improvements
Experience:
  • Experience setting up and maintaining Linux and windows server environments
  • Experience working with IaaC systems, ideally cloud formation
  • Experience working with AWS, ideally including Cloudformation, EC2, ECS, Lambda, Cloudwatch, RDS, S3, SQS
  • Experience working with Docker
  • Experience working with python
  • Experience working with git
Key Behaviors that we look for:
  • A positive mindset and a can-do attitude.
  • Attention to detail.
  • English Skills (ability to effectively communicate via written and spoken English).
  • You are constantly striving to learn new things and improve.
  • You follow industry news, regularly tinker with new technologies, and read books and blogs to keep your skills current.
  • You work well within a team environment and enjoy working in the office with your colleagues

Job Location: Sector 63, Noida

Job Type: Full-time

Schedule:
