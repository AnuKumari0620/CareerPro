Do I need a valid email id for registration?

Yes, you need a valid email id to register on CareerPro. The same email id will be used to communicate with you in the future. We value your privacy, and we hate spam as much as you do. We are not going to spam or sell your address to any third party. We still need your email account to be able to provide you with your job related information or for any other career related notifications you may choose.

Can you use the software on mobile and desktop?

With the help of the Education Management Information System, freshers can receive individual messages or group messages. It can be helpful when there is an important announcement to be made on short notice regarding jobs rescheduling or an upcoming deadline.
